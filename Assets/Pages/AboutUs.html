ABOUT US


Our Story


Calamba Legacy was founded with the passion to preserve and promote the rich cultural heritage of Calamba City, Laguna. Inspired by the city's vibrant history, traditions, and delicacies, we embarked on a journey to document and share Calamba’s unique stories with the world.




Mission


Our mission is to illuminate Calamba's cultural heritage by showcasing its folklore, traditions, culinary delights, and historical sites. We aim to inspire a deep appreciation for Calamba’s culture and encourage its preservation for future generations.



Vision


We envision Calamba Legacy as the go-to resource for exploring and learning about the city's past and present. By fostering a love for Calamba's cultural wealth, we strive to promote its legacy and ensure its stories endure.
